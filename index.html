Structural Design & Schedule B Services

Overview

We offer expert structural engineering solutions tailored to ensure safety, efficiency, and compliance with all building codes for both residential and light commercial projects.

Design Services

  • Complete Structural Design for New Buildings: Developing comprehensive structural plans that provide stability and durability for new constructions.
  • Renovation and Retrofit Designs: Creating innovative solutions to enhance and extend the lifespan of existing structures.
  • Foundation Design and Analysis: Engineering robust foundation systems tailored to site-specific conditions.

Technical Services

  • Schedule B Field Reviews: Conducting thorough on-site evaluations to verify compliance with design specifications and building codes.
  • Geotechnical Assessments and Upgrades: Evaluating soil and subsurface conditions to inform foundation design and ensure structural integrity.
  • Structural Condition Assessments: Inspecting and reporting on the current state of structures to inform maintenance and repair strategies.
  • Hazards Assessment: Identifying potential structural hazards and developing mitigation strategies to enhance safety and resilience.

Why Choose Our Structural Services?

  • ✓ Extensive Experience: Bringing a wealth of knowledge and a proven track record in delivering successful projects.
  • ✓ Comprehensive Documentation: Providing detailed reports and plans to facilitate smooth permit applications and project approvals.
  • ✓ Innovative Problem-Solving: Employing creative approaches to address complex structural challenges effectively.
  • ✓ Commitment to Safety and Compliance: Prioritizing adherence to all relevant building codes and standards, ensuring the utmost safety and reliability.
